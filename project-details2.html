I Will Survive

This project is a survival game where players must navigate diverse challenges to stay alive. The difficulty intensifies the longer players survive, with a variety of obstacles and enemies to overcome.

Features

  • Difficulty increases the longer the player plays.
  • Variety of obstacles and enemies
  • Engaging gameplay mechanics
  • Level/Upgrade System

Technologies Used

  • Unity
  • C#
  • Paint for Assets
